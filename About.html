SPACEKID OJ!

HITCHHIKERS GUIDE TO THE GALAXY!

Born and raised in Cape Town, South Africa MilkandOJ is a self taught multi disciplinary artist. In 2014 with a friend he started a blog called AGGRESSIVELY PROGRESSIV which was originally an outlet to express their art and hopefully share it with the world. Over the years the core of the idea still remains but it has now evolved to one that focuses on making positive change in his community through exhibiting the "OTHER"(something that is not commonly known or accepted in South African townships). Now a recruit at Umuzi Org He is now working on building a portfolio where he will be exhibiting works on film, illustrations and storytelling abilities
